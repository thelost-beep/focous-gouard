📖 How to Use FocusGuard Perfectly

1. Perfect Lighting 💡

Ensure your face is well-lit. Avoid sitting with bright windows directly behind you (backlighting), as it shadows your face and makes eye tracking difficult.

2. Camera Angle 📷

Position your camera roughly at eye level. If your laptop is too low, the AI might think you are always looking down (distracted). Do not stare straight up at the ceiling!

3. Smart Phone Detection 📱

FocusGuard actively scans for cell phones. If you hold a phone up to watch reels, it will instantly flag you as distracted—even if you are looking straight!

4. Fast Sleep Detection 😴

Gaze down naturally to read or write. But if your eyes close for more than 1 second, the alarm will instantly snap you awake.

5. Stay at your desk 🚫

If you start a session and walk away from your desk, the system will start screaming after a few seconds. Do not leave until you hit your goal!

6. Pomodoro Commitments ⏱️

Set how many minutes you want to focus before hitting "Start". Once you reach your goal, a Break Mode screen will appear so you can safely relax off-camera!
